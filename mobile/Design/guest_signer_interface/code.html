Consulting Services Agreement

This Consulting Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of this 14th day of October, 2023, by and between Global Tech Solutions Inc. ("Client") and Jane Doe ("Consultant").

Consultant agrees to provide the following services: Software architecture review, security compliance audit, and backend optimization strategies (collectively, the "Services"). The Consultant shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner.

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue until the Services are completed, unless earlier terminated by either party upon written notice.

Consultant acknowledges that during the engagement, they may have access to confidential information. Consultant agrees to hold such information in strict confidence.
